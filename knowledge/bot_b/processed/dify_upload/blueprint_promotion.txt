[1. Blueprint Document Scope > 1.1 Background Introduction]

Promotion management consists of promotion planning and execution. 1) In promotion planning, the business team would set sales and gross margin targets and plan promotions. 2) After planning, the system admin team would maintain promotion and marketing toolset activities across different companies (e.g. Supermarket, Hypermarket, Savemore Market,) or stores in the system and the promotions would take effect in the store so that customers can get a discount and a satisfying shopping experience.

Phase1 offers multiple promotion types together with marketing toolset system to support the business team to achieve various price strategies and promotion mechanics.

In the promotion planning stage, business needs to formulate price strategy and negotiate with vendors on the rebate agreement to build the promotion planning against each item category. But in DMALL promotion system and POS settlement process, DMALL directly records the promotion discount as shared by the merchant, that is SM. And the data is passed to SM through the order information and then processed by SM uniformly.

In the promotion execution stage, the system admin team would set up promotion activities and submit to supervisor review. Once the promotion activity is approved, its status would become ‘Approved’ automatically. When approved, the promotion will be executed normally during the validity period.

For any ongoing promotion activity, the system admin can terminate the activity any time and the termination will take effect instantly.

[3. Function Description > 3.2 Promotion Type > 3.2.3 Bank Promotion Barcode]

1. AS-IS Overview

SM supports tender discount.

2.To-Be Overview

DMALL supports tender discount, via bank promotion barcode.

3.To-Be Flow

4.TO-BE Description

For SM's "Promo per tender" promotional format, such as "Get FREE Item with a minimum grocery item worth P3,000 using BDO Credit Card E-VOUCHER PROMO", DMALL will support the Tender discount/ bank promotion to correspond to it. Operators need to configure a regular promotion with a bank promotion, and configure tender type. For example, create the promotion as "Get FREE Item with a minimum order worth P3,000" (it's a basket promotion), and correspond it to a tender type and barcode that can be downloaded.

Then, the promotion creator will send the barcode and promotion information to the store cashiers.

In the POS settlement process, the cashier can manually remind the customer that they can enjoy a discount if they pay with a specified tender type when the customer’s order may hit the tender discount promotion(e.g., order reach P3,000). If the customer agrees, the cashier can scan the barcode on the POS, and the system will try to hit the promotion and provide the customer with a discount. When the customer pays, the cashier supervises the customer to use the agreed payment method(e.g., BDO card). If the customer refuses to use the specified payment method(e.g., BDO card) at this time, the cashier will roll back the settlement process, cancel the promotion corresponding to the B, and settle again.

The management of bank promotion barcodes can be viewed in the Promotion configuration below (3.5.2.1).

[3. Function Description > 3.2 Promotion Type > 3.2.6 Double Receipt Freebie]

1. AS-IS Overview

In addition to the freebies normally acquired and redeemed through promotional activities (such as 'Buy X Get Y') at the Point of Sale (POS), SM also handle a kind of Special Managed Freebies, These freebies, due to theft prevention, vendor considerations, etc., are managed in a dedicated area, called redemption booth, within the store. Customers need to collect them in redemption booth with a tailend receipt after their transaction.

2.To-Be Overview

DMALL aims to accomplish the goal of this kind of freebies and their inventory management through the product form of "double receipt freebie" in Marketing toolset system, you can view the detail in 3.5.2.2 below.

3.To-Be Flow

4.TO-BE Description

DMALL supports the marketing strategy of "double receipt freebie",  where paper vouchers carry information about freebies. These vouchers are printed on the tailend receipt after POS settlement. Upon receiving a "double receipt freebie", customers can use it to collect freebies in the redemption booth.

The operational page for creating  "double receipt freebie" is detailed in section 3.5.2.2.

Meanwhile, it's important to note that "double receipt freebie" code need to be input manually by SM’s operator or manager in redemption booth. A common voucher style is depicted in the image below. Moreover, DMALL's system supports a degree of style management for the display of voucher. If SM has specific style requirements, further discussions and confirmations can be conducted.

When an order is completed on the POS, the marketing toolset system listens to POS order data to determine if the conditions for the "double receipt freebie" award type are met. These include reaching the marketing activity threshold (like buying specific articles or reaching a certain amount) and whether there’s enough remaining freebie vouchers. If the activity is hit, a tailend receipt is printed with a unique double receipt freebie code.

Creation：

You can learn more about this in the Promotion Management section below.

Approval flow：

Approval flow configuration: DMALL's task system supports creating a corresponding Flowchart for SM's promotion approval. The Flowchart allows for multiple approval flows based on conditions. The approver can approve or reject the promotion activity.

If approved, the promotion activity can be executed normally.

In the pending review status, if the creator can set the promotion activity to a voided status, and then there is no need to approve it again.

You can learn more about this in the Promotion Management section below.

4.TO-BE Description

For each promotion type, each article will only hit at most one promotion activity in the pricing process. The rules for hitting which activity include the Best offer rule (BOR), Normal priority rule (NPR), Special priority rule (SPR) shown in the figure above. And when an article hits a promotion activity of one type, whether it can still hit the promotion activities of subsequent types, the rules are shown in the table below:

Unit promo | Buy x get Y | Match promo | Condition promo | Basket promo | Payment Promo
Unit promo | BOR | Config | √
Buy x get Y | NPR | Config | √
Match promo | NPR | × | √
Condition promo | SPR | √
Basket promo | SPR | √
Payment Promo | NPR

“√”（tick） :means that when the article hits other types of promotion activities, it can still hit this type of promotion.

“×” （cross）:means that if the article has already hit the match promotion, it cannot hit the condition promotion again.

Config:Configurable, means that the promotion activity can be configured to allow or not allow the article to hit this activity when it hits the previous types of promotion activities.

BOR: Best offer rule. If the priorities are different, higher priority will be applied; If the priorities are the same, the best offer rule will be applied.

The priority of the general promotion activity can be customized and configured. The enumeration values include very high, high, normal, low, and very low.

NPR: Normal priority rule. If the priorities are different, higher priority will be applied; If the priorities are the same, the last created promotion will be chosen.

4.TO-BE Description

In DMALL system, Voucher, E-vouchers, next purchase coupon, or bank promotion

will correspond to specific promotion, each order can support multiple vouchers, the order pricing logic with vouchers is basically the same as order with general promotions, the difference is: In DMALL’s system, promotion activities corresponding voucher has a higher priority than others without voucher of the same promotion type. For example, if the promotion corresponding to the voucher is Buy A get B, and there is another promotion which is Buy A get C, and if the order contains articles A, B, and C, it will hit Buy A get B only, but not Buy A get C.

In the DMALL pricing rules, an article can generally only hit one promotion under the same promotion type. So in this solution, if article A has a general unit promotion: Buy A get $5 off and a voucher: Buy A get $8 off, then in the order, article A may only hit the voucher,and get $8 off, not get $(8+5) off.

[3. Function Description > 3.7 POS Order Settlement & Pricing Execution Process > 3.7.1 POS Execution Process with Marketing Toolset awarding]

1. AS-IS Overview

SM supports the possibility of hitting promotions at the time of POS settlement and issuing points to customers.

2.To-Be Overview

DMALL supports the possibility of hitting promotions at the time of POS settlement and issuing points to customers. And DMALL will call the API of SM to issue or reclaim. At the same time, DMALL can issue other kinds of marketing rewards like Next purchase coupon, Double receipt freebie, Advertising.

3.To-Be Flow

4.TO-BE Description

Promotion pricing: Promotion system will judge the hit of promotion activities based on articles and activity info, and then calculate the price of order.

Points awarded:

After payment, DMALL will calculate the points numbers that should be issued based on basic points rules, and then call SM's Points Awarding interface to issue membership the points.

And if the order hits the points reward rule, DMALL Marketing Toolset system will call SM's Points Awarding interface to issue membership extra points and N times points, and print corresponding info on the tailend receipt.

Points awarding reversal:

If order are returned or refund on POS, DMALL will call SM’s Points Awarding Reversal API, pass in the membership, order and refund information. Then SM reclaims the points.

Notes: As above, the points awarding and reversal requires real-time calling of SM’s interface. When the store network is error, this process can not be performed. Therefore, points can not be issued in this case.

Next purchase coupon, Double receipt freebie, Advertising awarded on tailend reiceipt: After payment, DMALL Marketing Toolset system will monitor order information. If the order qualifies for marketing activities with relevant reward types, the Marketing Toolset system will print the corresponding reward information on the tailend receipt. These reward types, in addition to the previously mentioned points, also include Next purchase coupon, Double receipt freebie, Advertising.

4.TO-BE Description

DMALL POS:

E-voucher code: The E-voucher code can be entered or scanned at the POS, and DMALL's promotion system can judge its validity and use the code to identify the corresponding promotion activity, enabling the hitting and pricing of the promotion.

Calculating order: As described in Section 3.6.4 above,, if voucher and regular promotion triggers the same type of promo, the voucher, including E-voucher and voucher on tailend receipt, always has higher priority as it's the discount that customers actively requested, making it more likely to be hit. And multiple Voucher codes can be used in one order, which will be converted into corresponding promotion activities for pricing. The pricing detail can be seen above section of pricing logic with Promotion voucher.

SM should provide the following APIs:

E-voucher redeem interface: When the payment is completed at the POS, DMALL's promotion system will invalidate the E-voucher code used in the transaction and call this interface in real-time, passing the code to SM. SM will then mark the corresponding E-voucher as redeemed on the customer's app.

Note: In DMALL's promotion system, if an E-voucher batch corresponding to a promotion activity ends or terminates early, DMALL will also call the E-voucher redeem interface to invalidate these codes. Therefore, this interface needs to support batch calls and distinguish between different calling scenarios.

E-voucher reversal interface: In case of order cancellation at the POS, DMALL's promotion system will re-enable the E-voucher code used in the transaction and call this interface, passing the transaction information to SM. SM will restore the corresponding E-voucher on the customer's app.

SM legacy POS:

On the SM legacy POS, E-vouchers can still be used. However, since the validity of E-voucher codes is centrally managed by DMALL's promotion system, DMALL will provide the following APIs to SM:

E-voucher Code Validity Check API: By providing the E-voucher code, this API will determine whether the code is in a valid state.

E-voucher Code Reversal on SM Side API: In case of order cancellation on the SM legacy POS and the need to restore the codes used in the order, this API is called to inform DMALL about the restoration status of the code.

As above, E-vouchers can not be used when POS has no network connection , since the APIs between SM and DMALL are not available when there is no internet connection.

POS Offline: As mentioned above, if the POS network is offline, points awarding and reversal, as well as the use of E-vouchers, promotion activities involving the selection of articles in vendor fields, store-level article purchase limitation, will be interrupted. However, other functions such as the hitting and pricing of promotions, and the printing of vouchers, Freebies, and Paper coupons on the tailend receipt, will not be immediately affected. Nevertheless, if the network remains offline for an extended period, issues may arise as the stores won't receive updated data from the Promotion and Marketing Toolset systems. Therefore, if the POS network is offline, prompt action by the store staff is still necessary. Once the network connection is restored, the Promotion system will also return to normal without additional operation.

For issues like abnormal execution, Pricing of Promotion Activities, or potential problems with store POS systems being offline, DMALL will provide more detailed and explicit guidance documents before delivering the project to SM.
